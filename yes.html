It is considered an epitome of beauty and passion.

Roses have been used as metaphors by renowned authors and poets over the centuries.

Depending on their color, roses are said to have different meanings.

Pink Roses are a symbol of youth and femininity,

White Roses symbolize purity, innocence and peace.

Black Roses are used as metaphors for death while yellow roses are associated with friendship, freedom and even jealousy.
